About

Planned for Summer 2016, the Great Suffolk Cycle Ride is a multi stage charity cycling event organised by Sunrise Funding (a charity specifically founded to raise money for hospitals, clinics and other organisation treating or supporting those with Cancer. The Great Suffolk Cycle Ride will either pass through or near every major population center in Suffolk, from Lowestoft to Ipswich, Felixstowe to Sudbury and many more.
